The pros and cons of Intermittent Fasting

What is intermittent fasting?

With intermittent fasting, you only eat during a specific time of the day.It is not a diet rather a pettern of eating that is followed.

Changes that occur within you while fasting:

  1. Human Growth Hormone This growth hormone increases and has benifits for fat loss and muscle gain
  2. Insulin sensitivity improves and insulin levels drop dramatically.
  3. Cellular Repair allows cells to digets and remove old and dysfunctional proteins that build up inside cells.
  4. Gene Expression Changes happen that help protection againt disease
