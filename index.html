The pros and cons of Intermittent Fasting

What is intermittent fasting?

With intermittent fasting, you only eat during a specific time of the day.It is not a diet rather a pattern of eating that is followed.

Changes that occur within you while fasting:

  1. Human Growth Hormone This growth hormone increases and has benefits for fat loss and muscle gain
  2. Insulin sensitivity improves and insulin levels drop dramatically.
  3. Cellular Repair allows cells to digest and remove old and dysfunctional proteins that build up inside cells.
  4. Gene Expression Changes happen that help protection againt disease
Types of Fasting
16:8 18:6 Alternate Day Warrior Fasting
Allows you to eat for 8 hours and fast for 16 Allows you to eat for 6 hours and fast for 18 Fasting every other day, includes not eating at all or small amounts Eating small amounts of raw fruits and vegetables and one large meal at night

Cons of Fasting

  1. Lightheadedness and headaches are possible side effects
  2. Hunger and Cravings
  3. Irritability and other mood chnages
  4. Fatigue and low energy
  5. Dehydration
